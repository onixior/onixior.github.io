background...

during the winter seasons, warming down up with a fresh plate of muffins with a glass of milk is key to enjoying the colder months. this recipe was recently discovered to me, and i've already been obsessed with making these blueberry muffins!
coating these muffins with chocolate drizzling adds to the sweet and melty flavor!


sharing it with loved ones is what makes it special! - my mother!

tips and tricks!

  • keep the wet and dry ingredients seperately!
  • toss the blueberries in flour before adding them to the batter to prevent batter sinking
  • make sure the muffin batter is thick!! making them more filling will add to the recipe and enhance its sweetness!

the recipe!

muffin recipe!
ingredients! measurements! instructions!
all-purpose flour 1 1/2 cups whisk flour with other ingredients (listed below) in a large bowl!
white sugar 3/4 cup combine with flour and other ingredients in a large bowl (adding on from previous recipe)
baking powder 2 teaspoons combine with other dry ingredients (adding on from prevuious recipe)
salt 1/2 teaspoon combine with previous ingredients in a large bowl (adding on from previous recipe step
vegetable oil 1/3 cup pour the oil into a small liquid measuring cup!
egg 1 large egg add egg from previous step!
egg whites 5 large eggs! add the egg whites to the butter + sugar mix for about two minutes before adding other materials!
sour cream or plain yogurt 1/3 cup add sour cream with vanilla extract to the egg + butter + sugar mix!
pure vanilla extract 2 teaspoons add with sour cream as explained in the previous step!
whole milk 1/2 cup slowly pour once all dry ingredients and butter + vanilla extract mix are present! after, whisk in 1/2 cup of room-temperature reduced strawberry puree.
prep time! 1 hour and 30 minutes
number of servings! 0-12 people


table for the cream cheese frosting!

ingredients! measurements! methods!
freeze-dried strawberries 1 cup make puree out of strawberry, and let cool. simmer until 1/2 cup left.
cream cheese 8 ounces beat cream cheese in large bowl until smooth and creamy.
unsalted butter 1/2 cup after you add in the cream cheese, add in butter until combined with the strawberries and cream cheese!
confectioners' sugar 3 cups add in the sugar after beating of butter is done!
milk 1-2 tablespoons add with the sugar from previous step!
pure vanilla extract 1 teaspoon add with previous ingredients from prior two steps!
salt just to taste. taste, then add a pinch of salt if needed.
make time 20 minutes
serving size! 3 cups
