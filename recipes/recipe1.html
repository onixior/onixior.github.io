background...

this recipe goes far back into my family's history! whenever spring came around throughout the year, i helped my mother bake this special cake. it sort of became a tradition, and now, we invite close family friends and relatives over to share this special recipe.
sharing the warmthness of spring, this cake has many connections to good, nostaglic, and sentimental memories.


sharing it with loved ones is what makes it special! - my mother!

tips and tricks!

  • remember to use fresh strawberries! leave them in a baking soda - honey water mixture, and leave to sit for at least a whole day. this enchances the flavor!
  • leave the cake to sit, and then decorate! decorating while hot leads to the toppings and cream melting.
  • to prevent the strawberries from melting onto the cake, spread a thin layer of chocolate ganache before applying the fruits! the chocolate acts as a sealant !

the recipe!

puree + cake
ingredients! measurements! instructions!
strawberry puree 1 pound (454g) fresh strawberries, rinsed and left to dry make the puree first, and let cool. simmer over medium-low hear until about 1/2 cup is left. allow it to return to room temperature before adding back into cake batter!
cake flour 2 and 1/2 cups whisk the cake flour with other ingredients to create the batter!
baking poweder 2 teaspoons whisk with cake flour and other ingredients to form batter!
salt 1 teaspoon whisk with other ingredients to form batter!
unsalted butter 3/4 cup (1/2 tbsp) beat room temperature butter and sugar until smooth and creamed for about two minutes! add in other ingredients after time, and add in puree from earlier.
granulated sugar 1 and 3/4 cups mix with butter from previous ingredient!
egg whites 5 large eggs! add the egg whites to the butter + sugar mix for about two minutes before adding other materials!
sour cream or plain yogurt 1/3 cup add sour cream with vanilla extract to the egg + butter + sugar mix!
pure vanilla extract 2 teaspoons add with sour cream as explained in the previous step!
whole milk 1/2 cup slowly pour once all dry ingredients and butter + vanilla extract mix are present! after, whisk in 1/2 cup of room-temperature reduced strawberry puree.
